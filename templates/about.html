About SkyFarm

SkyFarm is dedicated to empowering farmers in Uganda and beyond by providing accessible, technology-driven agricultural solutions. Our mission is to cultivate a greener future through sustainable farming practices and innovative tools tailored to local needs.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every farmer has the knowledge and resources to thrive. By bridging traditional farming with modern technology, SkyFarm delivers actionable advice to enhance soil health, boost crop yields, and promote sustainability.

Our Features

  • USSD Accessibility: Access farming advice via SMS/USSD on any phone, ensuring inclusivity for all farmers.
  • Tailored Recommendations: Get personalized advice based on your district, crop, and local conditions.
  • Sustainable Practices: Learn eco-friendly methods to improve soil health and crop resilience.
  • Community Support: Connect with a network of farmers and experts to share knowledge and grow together.
